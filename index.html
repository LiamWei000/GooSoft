About GooSoft

GooSoft is a global creative technology initiative founded by Liam Wei (AEIOU 05). Our mission is to build intuitive, modular, and open source editor platforms that redefine digital creativity.

Vision

To become a global pioneer in open source creative technology that empowers innovation without boundaries.

Mission

  • Develop modular and accessible editor platforms
  • Support global collaboration through open source principles
  • Empower creators from beginner to professional level
